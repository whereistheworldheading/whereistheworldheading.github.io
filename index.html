Where is the world heading ?

Military spending

Today the military spendings are 10x higher than by the end of the WWII.

The military spending worldwide has been rising since the World War 2. There was a slight decrease after the Cold War, then the spendings resumed their rise.

Military conflicts casualities by type of conflict

The interstate armed conflicts are disappearing

Some explaining factors are :
- The nuclear dissuasion
- The devastating cost for both parties
- The world sanctions

Number of ongoing conflicts by category

Other than civil wars, the other types of conflicts have been disappearing.

There is a rise of intrastate conflicts for political or identity reasons.

There is also a rise of new forms of warfare :
- Cyberwars : comprising intellectual property theft, hacking key infrastructures, or distabilising major networks
- Terrorism
- Propaganda which is more targeted and at a larger scale, using fake news for example

Refugee population

The trend was reversed on 2005, and the refugee population has been rising since then.

The global situation has worsened in 2016 :
- 10.3 million people were newly displaced (20 displacements every minute)
- 10 million people were stateless or at risk of stateless
- 50 % of the refugee population were childern - 552, 200 refugees returned - 2.9 million refugees were hosted by Turkey, which hosts the largest number of refugees worldwide
